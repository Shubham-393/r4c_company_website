Our Research Domains

Pioneering advancements at the intersection of intelligent systems, defense, and aerospace.

At R4C Tech, our research and technological developments focus on creating intelligent solutions for complex defense and aerospace challenges. Through continuous innovation and multidisciplinary approaches, we develop systems that enhance situational awareness, threat detection, and response capabilities.

Rocket Launch Detection

Development of systems capable of real-time detection and analysis of rocket launches to enhance situational awareness and response strategies.

  • Early warning systems
  • Trajectory analysis algorithms
  • Multi-sensor fusion technology
  • Real-time threat assessment

Seismic & Infrasound Monitoring

Integration of seismic and infrasound sensor networks for monitoring and analyzing ground-based and atmospheric events.

  • Distributed sensor networks
  • Signal processing algorithms
  • Event characterization systems
  • Low-frequency acoustic analysis

Cyber-Physical Threat Detection

Creation of intelligent systems to identify and mitigate threats that span both cyber and physical domains, ensuring comprehensive security solutions.

  • Anomaly detection systems
  • AI-driven security monitoring
  • Integrated threat response
  • Critical infrastructure protection

Ground Vehicle Systems

Design and development of advanced systems for monitoring and controlling ground vehicles, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.

  • Autonomous navigation
  • Fleet management solutions
  • Predictive maintenance systems
  • Real-time telemetry

UAV/Satellite Protection

Implementation of protective measures and systems for unmanned aerial vehicles and satellites to safeguard against potential threats.

  • Anti-jamming technologies
  • Secure communication protocols
  • Threat evasion systems
  • Resilient control architecture

Battle Management Tools

Development of tools for effective battle management, including event processing workflows and space asset monitoring.

  • Command and control systems
  • Decision support frameworks
  • Real-time asset tracking
  • Situational awareness platforms

R&D Tools

Advancement of research and development tools focusing on machine learning algorithms, real-time data fusion, and systems engineering.

  • AI model development platforms
  • Data fusion frameworks
  • Simulation environments
  • Systems engineering tools

Johnson, S., Wilson, J., & Rodriguez, E.

Journal of Aerospace Defense Systems, Vol. 35, 2023

This paper presents novel approaches to improve the accuracy and response time of rocket launch detection systems through the integration of multiple sensor modalities, including infrared, radar, and acoustic sensors.

International Conference on Defense Technology, 2022
